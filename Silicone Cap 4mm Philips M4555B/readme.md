## Easy Care Adult M4555B - Philips

Designfiles to make a replacement cap for Easy Care Adult M4555B - Philips. Blood pressure cuff tube.


### Fabrication guide

#### Prerequisites
Two part Platinumcure silicone, Shore A 20-30

#### Steps

Print mold
Mix silicone
Pour into mold
Take out cured silicone
Cut to get caps
